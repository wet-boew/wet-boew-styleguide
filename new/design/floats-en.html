Float options

Float an element to the left or right with a class. 

<div class="pull-left">...</div>
<div class="pull-right">...</div>

Example

Pulled right A

Pulled right B

<div class="row">
  <div class="col-md-4 pull-right">
    <div class="well">
      <p>Pulled right A</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="col-md-4 pull-right">
    <div class="well">
      <p>Pulled right B</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Clearing floats

Clear the float on any element with the .clearfix class. 

<div class="clearfix">...</div>

Example

Pulled right A

Pulled right B

<div class="row">
  <div class="col-md-4 pull-right">
    <div class="well">
      <p>Pulled right A</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="clearfix"></div>
  <div class="col-md-4 pull-right">
    <div class="well">
      <p>Pulled right B</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
